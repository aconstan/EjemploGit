Excellence Scholarship:
Given to the most brilliant students in Mexican high schools, covers 90% of tuition expenses.

Academic Scholarship and Loan:
Given to academic high achievers whose economic condition does not allow them to cover the complete tuition expenses. They have to pay for the loan later.
 
Sports Scholarship-Credit:
Given to students that distinguish themselves for their academic and athletic performance.

Líderes del Mañana scholarship:
Students which are leaders with little economic resources to pay for higher education. It covers 100% of course fees.
